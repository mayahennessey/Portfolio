All about Maya Hennessey

Hi, My name is Maya Hennessey! I am a student filmmaker currently attending Southington High School and The Greater Hartford Academy of The Arts where I specialize in creative writing and media arts. My background in scriptwriting, directing, editing, camera techniques, sound design, color detail, and acting have allowed me to have extensive knowledge in every aspect of film to bring to my films and others. I am passionate about bringing up tough topics that people don't usually like to talking about; such as mental health, bullying, and anxiety. I like stories that focus on conveying an important feeling rather than a definive story. Insetad of wondering, "what was my film about?"" I challenge my audience is challenged to ask "how did my film make me feel?" The past two summers, I attended Maine Media's 2 Week Youth Filmmakers Program and Emerson College's 5-Week Young Digital Filmmakers Program where I continued to expand my knowledge and explore other positions on set. These opportunities have allowed me to realize what kind of role I want to have in the film industry and what aspects of film I want to focus on in college and my career.
Outside of film, I was a competitive dancer for over six years and brought home three national championships. I have also performed in multiple theatre performances. Recently, I was in The Greater Hartford Academy of The Arts Spring Musical "A Chorus Line" which recieved three awards from the Connecticut Halo Awards: Best Feature Dancer In A Musical, Best Performance By An Ensemble Cast In A Musical, and Best Classical Musical 2018. I played Connie Wong and performed in an ensemble group of 25 students. Currently, I am a member of the Connecticut Tap Collaborative where the company focuses on collaboration, improvisation, and the love of tap dance. I am an independent photographer and a videographer for John Canning Studios in Cheshire Connecticut. I also run two Youtube channels with over 40,000 subscibers on each. One is a personal lifestyle channel and another is a littlest pet shop channel for kids to watch and enjoy short series I create. I love being active in the community and having multiple hobbies, and I know the skills I learn from these activities will better prepare me for my career.
