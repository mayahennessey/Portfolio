My career started at the young age of ten years old, and now six years later, I am continuing my filmmaking career with my job online. !

My name is Maya Hennessey. I am a sixteen year-old attending Southington High School and The Greater Hartford Academy of the arts studying writing and film. I have been working online with Youtube since I was thirteen and recently started creating short films to make profit from.
Ever since Maya was little, she has enjoyed making art. She performed in local plays and danced on a competition dance team for 7 years, before ending the experience to dedicate her time to film.
